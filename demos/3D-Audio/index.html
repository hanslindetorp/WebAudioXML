3D-audio

ObjectBasedAudio

In WebAudioXML there is an object called ObjectBasedAudio. With this object, you can place your sounds in a three dimensional virtual space using binaural-technology. You specify the placement with the attributes positionX positionY and positionZ. The units can be thought of as 'meter', but is not an absolute scale. All sounds are heard in relation to a 'listener' that can be positioned and turned in any direction. Click the 'Music and Mixer' button to try it. Activate the sounds and move the listener on the X and Z axis and the objects to hear the result. Click on the grid to turn the listener towards the pointer. The values for the listener are shown below the grid.
Note! ObjectBasedAudio works best in MONO.

AmbientAudio

I WebAudioXML there is also an AmbientAudio-object. It's made for making it easy to add looped background audio. The loops are 'intelligent' and make a crossfade on each repeat to make it smooth.
